Bread halwa

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • sugar
  • elachi
  • oil
  • milk

Recipe

  1. Cut 6–8 bread slices into pieces and shallow fry in ghee until golden.
  2. Make sugar syrup by boiling 1 cup sugar with 1/2 cup water.
  3. Add 1 cup milk to the syrup and bring to a boil.
  4. Mix fried bread into the milk and cook until soft.
  5. Add cardamom powder and optional saffron or rose water.
  6. Stir until the mixture thickens.
  7. Garnish with ghee-fried nuts and serve warm.

GulabJamun

Ingredients

  • flour
  • sugar
  • Flour
  • oil
  • milk

Recipe

  1. Prepare dough using milk powder, all-purpose flour, and ghee
  2. shape into small balls.
  3. Deep fry the balls on low heat until golden brown.
  4. Make sugar syrup by boiling 1 cup sugar with 1.5 cups water and cardamom.
  5. Add fried balls to warm syrup and let them soak for 2–3 hours.
  6. Serve warm or chilled.

Burfi

Ingredients

  • sugar
  • Almonds
  • Ghee
  • milk

Recipe

  1. Roast 1 cup gram flour (or desired flour) in ghee until aromatic.
  2. Mix sugar and water to make a one-string consistency syrup.
  3. Add the syrup to the roasted flour and mix well.
  4. Cook until the mixture thickens and leaves the sides of the pan.
  5. Pour into a greased tray, flatten, and let it set.
  6. Cut into pieces and serve.
